# Competitive Intelligence Report: mock-competitor.com vs. SaaSify Inc.

## Executive Summary
This report presents a comprehensive competitive analysis of the pricing and product structures of **mock-competitor.com** against **SaaSify Inc.** (our standard plans). 

Our analysis reveals that the competitor adopts an aggressive pricing strategy targeted at highly cost-conscious small-to-medium businesses, setting their starter price point at $10/user/month and their mid-tier at $30/user/month. While SaaSify Inc. provides significantly higher value in terms of features—such as workflow automation in our Starter tier, and integrations, 24/7 support, and SLAs in our Professional tier—there is a major pricing gap. Specifically, teams with 6 to 25 users face a massive price jump under SaaSify's current structures, creating an opportunity for mock-competitor.com to capture market share.

## Price Differentials (Mathematical Analysis)

* **Entry-Level (Starter vs. Starter):**
  * **Monthly Billing:** SaaSify's Starter plan ($15) is **$5 (50%) more expensive** per user/month than the competitor's Starter plan ($10).
  * **Annual Billing Equivalent:** SaaSify's annual equivalent ($12) is still **$2 (20%) more expensive** than the competitor's monthly flat rate.
* **Mid-Tier (Growth vs. Professional):**
  * **Monthly Billing:** SaaSify's Professional plan ($49) is **$19 (63.3%) more expensive** per user/month than the competitor's Growth plan ($30).
  * **Annual Billing Equivalent:** SaaSify's annual equivalent ($39) is **$9 (30%) more expensive** than the competitor's monthly flat rate.

## Identified Feature Gaps

### SaaSify Inc. Strengths (Competitor Gaps)
1. **Automation & Reporting at Entry Level:** Our Starter plan offers **core workflow automation** and **basic dashboard reporting**, whereas the competitor only offers basic "core task management" at this tier.
2. **Higher User Caps on Mid-Tier:** Our Professional tier supports up to **50 users**, compared to the competitor's cap of **25 users** on their Growth plan.
3. **Enterprise-Grade Professional Tier:** SaaSify offers **custom integrations**, **24/7 priority support**, and an **SLA uptime guarantee** in the Professional tier. The competitor locks SLA support entirely behind their custom-priced Enterprise wall and only offers priority *email* support at the mid-tier.

### Competitor Strengths (SaaSify Gaps)
1. **Intermediate Scaling Path:** The competitor offers a smooth intermediate stepping stone for teams growing past 5 users. A SaaSify customer growing to 6 users is forced to jump from **$15/user/month** (Starter) to **$49/user/month** (Professional)—a **226.7% price increase** per user. Under the competitor's model, they would only move from $10 to $30.

---

## Strategic Recommendations

1. **Leverage and Market Feature Superiority:** We must aggressively highlight our advanced feature set (automation and reporting) at the Starter level to justify the extra $5/month.
2. **Introduce a Mid-Tier "Growth" Plan:** To capture the 6–25 user segment without forcing them to jump to the $49/user/month Professional tier, we recommend introducing a **"Growth"** plan at **$29/user/month billed monthly** (or **$24/user/month billed annually**).
3. **Promote the Annual Discount:** Push annual billing heavily for the Starter plan to align SaaSify's price ($12 equivalent) closer to the competitor's entry point ($10), neutralizing their pricing advantage while securing longer contract commitments.
